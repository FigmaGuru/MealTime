Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MealTime ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the MealTime mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Meal & Plan Data: Recipes, ingredients, meal plans, and shopping lists you create within the app are stored in our cloud database (Firebase Firestore).
  • Usage Data: Basic usage information such as app interactions, feature usage, and crash reports may be collected to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the MealTime Service.
  • Sync your meals and meal plans across devices.
  • Generate AI-powered meal suggestions and nutrition estimates using OpenAI's API. Your meal data may be sent to OpenAI solely to generate these suggestions.
  • Respond to your questions or support requests.
  • Improve the app's features and user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

MealTime uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In – Used for account authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI – Used to generate meal suggestions and nutrition information. OpenAI Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore), which uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and meal data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

6. Children's Privacy

MealTime is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at markydoyle@gmail.com.
